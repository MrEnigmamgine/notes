# Hertz

Hertz is a measure of frequency, $\text{occurrences}/{time}$. One hertz is 1 occurrence per [[Second]].

$$
\begin{align*}
1\: \text{Hertz} &= 1\,Hz \\
&= 1/s \\
&= \frac{{\Delta \nu}_{Cs}}{9,192,631,770}
\end{align*}
$$
