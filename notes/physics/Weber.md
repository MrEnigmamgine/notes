The weber is a unit of [[magnetic flux]]. It can be defined in terms of [[Faraday's Law]], which observes that a change in flux of one weber per [[Second]] will induce one [[Volt]] of potential.

$$
1V = 1 \frac{Wb}{s}
$$

Therefore

$$
1\ Wb = 1 V s
$$
